Introduction

Sea and Salt Magazine

Sea and Salt is a web magazine showcasing bakeries along the California coast. Not only does the magazine highlight famous bakeries, stylish bakeware, and secret recipes, but also includes stunning imagery of the outdoors. Articles include stories and how-to's from famous bakers, as well as travel guides for those looking to visit local bakeries or sightsee.
