Must-See Bakeries in Carmel-by-the-Sea

Carmel-by-the-Sea, California is paradise for people who love the beach, fine wines and quaint downtown areas. It's also paradise for people who adore delectable baked goods. Who doesn't, anyway? Visitors who are planning a trip to the idyllic city on California's Central Coast will have a blast enjoying all of their options in tasty bakeries.

With a sweet treat in hand, visiting one of Carmel's beaches is a must. At the end of Ocean Avenue, Carmel Beach is one of the most breathtaking spots on California's Central Coast. From morning to night Carmel Beach attracts visitors and locals alike to take in the majestic scenery. Additionally, fans of delectable dining have no complaints when they visit gorgeous Carmel-by-the-Sea in California. Great Italian dining options in Carmel are plentiful. Finally, don't forget to check out the beautiful Carmel Mission and other landmarks on your trip. Article by whatsupmonterey.com.
